# telecom-analysis
## introduction
ConnectaTel, a telecommunications company operating in Mexico and Colombia.
The company's objective is to identify usage patterns, detect atypical behaviour and understand which customer segments have different needs, in order to optimise its commercial offering and improve the user experience.
### datasets
--- plans.csv 
--- users.csv 
--- usage.csv

## Executive analysis
## ⚠️ Problems detected in the data

--- First, there is a column that is in the wrong format (churn_date), as it should be in Boolean format.
--- In my opinion, the number of calls column can be removed and only the number of minutes per call column should be used, as it would be based on the minutes that the user uses in their plans rather than the number of times they make a call.

## 🔍 Segments by age

--- It was identified that the adult age group (between 30 and 60 years old) accounts for 50% of users who have a plan with ConnectaTel.
--- Low participation of young users, which may be due to the possible use of prepaid plans or OTT services (WhatsApp, streaming, etc.).

## 📊 Segments by Level of Use

--- Most customers have average usage, meaning that most use the service regularly but not intensively.
--- There are very few high-consumption users, but these users tend to be the most valuable economically.

## 💡 Recommendations

--- Marketing campaigns should focus primarily on adults, as they represent the main source of revenue.
--- Create affordable plans or student plans, or promotions for mobile data or social media.
--- As there is regular use of the plans, plan upgrades can be offered, encouraging greater consumption with data packages, streaming services, or bundles.
--- Monitor low-consumption users, as they may represent less committed customers or customers who may switch operators. To retain them, promotions, data bonuses, or discounts on upgrades can be offered, which is what many companies are doing now.
